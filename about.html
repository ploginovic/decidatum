About Decidatum

Better decisions, built on evidence.

At Decidatum, we help organisations make complex decisions with confidence. We combine rigorous research, advanced analytics, and domain expertise to transform fragmented information into clear, actionable intelligence.

Whether evaluating healthcare technologies, informing policy, assessing markets, or designing evidence strategies, we deliver answers that stand up to scrutiny.

Why we exist

Important decisions are often made under pressure, with incomplete information.

Our mission is simple: replace uncertainty with evidence. Rather than offering generic consultancy, we build bespoke evidence frameworks using scientific literature, real-world data, public datasets, advanced analytics, and expert insight.

Evidence-first by design

We focus on what the evidence shows, what it does not show, and what decision-makers should do next.

Built for scrutiny

Our outputs are structured to be explainable, traceable, and useful in real-world decision settings.

Evidence mapping

We translate dispersed research and data into a usable structure that decision-makers can act on with confidence.

What makes us different

Clinical expertise meets data science.

Decidatum was founded by professionals working at the intersection of medicine, research, and analytics. We understand both clinical practice and the technical challenge of extracting meaningful insight from complex data.

Exceptional speed without compromising quality

By combining experienced researchers with modern analytical workflows and AI-assisted evidence synthesis, we deliver robust outputs on timelines that would traditionally require much larger teams.

An international network of specialists

We draw on a growing network of analysts, clinicians, researchers, statisticians, economists, policy experts, and subject-matter specialists.

Research-first methodology

Every recommendation is traceable back to evidence, with transparent methods and clearly documented assumptions.

Our vision

The best decisions happen when scientific evidence, high-quality data, and human expertise work together.

Decidatum exists to provide organisations with clarity through evidence and research they can trust. We combine analytical excellence with practical recommendations that create measurable impact.
